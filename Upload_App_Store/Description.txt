YC Todo

YC Todo is a lightweight macOS menubar task and focus app designed for fast, distraction-free productivity.

The app lives entirely in the macOS menubar, allowing you to quickly create tasks, assign focus durations, and track progress without opening a full window or leaving your current workflow.

YC Todo is built for simplicity and speed:

Create and manage tasks directly from the menubar

Assign focus durations to each task

Track progress with a clean, minimal interface

Keep your workflow uncluttered and distraction-free

All data is stored locally on your device. YC Todo does not require an account, does not connect to external servers, and works fully offline.

Designed for macOS users who value focus, clarity, and a clean desktop experience.
